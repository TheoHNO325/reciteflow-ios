# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-19

## 1. Overview

`背过` ("the App") is a study and memorization tool. This Privacy Policy
describes what information may be processed when users access the web version,
mobile version, or related services.

## 2. Information You Provide

The App may process content that you choose to enter or upload, including:

- study materials in Markdown or text form
- memorization plans generated from your materials
- reminder times and local study preferences

## 3. How Information Is Used

Your information may be used to:

- generate memorization schedules and review cards
- display imported learning materials
- store reminder settings and local study progress
- improve app stability, debugging, and product quality

## 4. Local Storage

Some study data, reminder settings, and app preferences may be stored locally
on your device or in your browser so the App can restore your session the next
time you open it.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App may rely on third-party infrastructure or SDKs for hosting, app
distribution, notifications, analytics, or build delivery. Those providers may
process technical metadata according to their own policies.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose information only:

- with your direction or consent
- to service providers that help operate the App
- when required by law, legal process, or protection of rights

## 7. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on the user device or browser until removed by the
user, deleted by the app, or cleared through device/browser settings.

## 8. Children

The App is not knowingly directed to children under the age required by
applicable law without parental or guardian authorization.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no system can guarantee
absolute security.

## 10. Your Choices

You may stop using the App, remove local data, uninstall the mobile app, or
clear browser storage at any time.

## 11. Contact

For privacy questions, requests, or notices, please provide an official contact
email or support address before public release and add it to this policy.
